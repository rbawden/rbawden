About

I am currently in the third year of my PhD in computer science at Université Paris-Sud at the LIMSI-CNRS laboratory in Paris, France. My PhD subject is the machine translation of spontaneous dialogue, supervised by Sophie Rosset and Thomas Lavergne.

My previous research experience (see CV and Experience) in Natural Language Processing has been in dependency parsing with Sylvain Kahane (MoDyCo, Université Paris X), the relationship between dialogue acts and prosody with Frédéric Landragin (LaTTiCe-CNRS) and Chloé Clavel (Télécom Paris-Tech) and model selection for statistical syntactic parsing using an approach via boosting with Benoit Crabbé (Alpage, Université Paris VII).
